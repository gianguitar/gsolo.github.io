New Single "Stay" Available on All Streaming Platforms 8/27!



I'm excited to announce that my new single "Stay" will be available on all streaming platforms on August 27th! I wrote pieces of the lyrics to Stay in different areas of Los Angeles, hence why the lyrics are strung loosely. But the gist of the song is literally no secret, it’s a song about someone that is begging for their partner, lover or acquaintance to stay the night, instead of hitting the road. Whether it’d be for love, comfort, accompaniment, camaraderie or just not wanting to be alone, the ask is real. Sometimes, at this point in time, lasting friendships are made or broken. I wanted to capture all of those emotions. Please stay tuned for more info!

About

Los Angeles Singer-Songwriter Gian Solo was born in the Philippines and spent his early childhood days exploring the world of music, sifting through cassettes purchased at nearby record stores. Known for authentic songwriting, exceptional guitar proficiency, commanding live performances and lyrical candor, Gian’s music has been compared to a wide range of artists including Devendra Banhart, Simon & Garfunkel, Sublime and Mumford & Sons. Gian’s latest single, “Stay,” features warm vocals with honest, thoughtful lyrics, over a lush, beautiful acoustic and synth landscape. It’s a sad-folk song about a partner asking for their love to stay the night, instead of hitting the road or home, which could be a big ask, depending on the situation. Gian has previously toured, recorded and released music with various bands and artists and is now producing music on his own for release and licensing. As an avid supporter of social justice, equality, human rights and education about racism, Gian creates music that inspires, drives and encourages love, equality, humanity, positivity, solidarity, diversity, happiness and inclusivity. Gian is giving life and energy back through music. Gian currently supports Stop AAPI hate, Music through education programs, Black Lives Matter and Filipino advocate groups for social justice and equality.
